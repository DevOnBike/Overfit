# Acme Software — Support FAQ

## How do I reset my password?

Go to the sign-in page and click "Forgot password". Enter the email on your account and we send a reset link valid for 60 minutes. If the link expires, request a new one. Password resets do not affect your data or active sessions on other devices.

## How do I add a teammate to my account?

Account owners and admins can invite teammates from **Settings → Members → Invite**. Enter the teammate's email and pick a role (Admin, Editor, or Viewer). The invite is valid for 7 days. The number of members you can add depends on your plan: Starter is single-user, Business allows up to 25, Enterprise is unlimited.

## Why can't I export the audit log?

Audit-log export is available on the Business and Enterprise plans only. On the Starter plan the audit log is visible in the dashboard for the last 30 days but cannot be exported. Upgrading to Business unlocks CSV and JSON export of the full retained history.

## How do I enable single sign-on (SSO)?

SSO is an Enterprise-plan feature. It supports SAML 2.0 and OIDC. To enable it, an admin goes to **Settings → Security → SSO**, uploads the identity-provider metadata, and assigns a default role for newly provisioned users. Contact support if you need a guided setup; Enterprise customers can request a screen-share with an engineer.

## My invoice looks wrong — what do I do?

Check **Settings → Billing → Invoices** for the line-item breakdown first; proration on mid-cycle upgrades is the most common surprise. If you believe you were charged in error (for example a duplicate charge), contact support with the invoice number. Confirmed billing errors are refunded within 5 business days.

## Can I move my data to US hosting?

Data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt) by default. Business and Enterprise customers can request US hosting; the migration is scheduled with support and typically completes within one maintenance window. Starter plans are EU-only.

## How do I cancel?

Cancel any time from **Settings → Billing → Cancel subscription**. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period and stops future charges. See the Customer Policy Handbook for the refund rules that apply to your plan and region.

## Is my data used to train AI models?

No. Acme does not use customer content to train shared models, and does not sell customer data. On account deletion, customer content is permanently erased within 30 days.

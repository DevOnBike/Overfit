## Full description  (max 4000 chars)
```
OverThink runs a real AI language model entirely on your phone. No servers, no cloud, no account — your
conversations never leave your device.

Pick a model, type a message, and watch the answer stream in, fully offline. OverThink ships with a small
built-in model so it works the moment you install it, and you can load any GGUF model from your phone for
better quality.

WHY OVERTHINK
• 100% on-device — inference runs locally, nothing is uploaded.
• Truly private — no tracking, no analytics, no ads, no data collection.
• Works offline — no internet connection needed to chat.
• Bring your own model — load any GGUF model file (Qwen, Llama, Mistral and more).
• Streaming replies — tokens appear live as the model thinks.
• Lightweight & clean — a fast, distraction-free chat built for your phone.

HOW IT WORKS
OverThink uses Overfit, an open-source pure-C#/.NET inference engine that runs quantized language models
directly on your device's CPU — no Python, no native runtime, no server round-trips. Choose the bundled
starter model to try it instantly, or add a larger GGUF model from your storage for stronger answers.

PRIVACY FIRST
Nothing you type is sent anywhere. There are no user accounts and no telemetry. The app requests no
sensitive permissions — no location, contacts, camera, or microphone. Model files you pick stay on your
device. Full policy: https://devonbike.github.io/Overfit/overthink-privacy.html

NOTE
On-device AI depends on your phone's hardware — larger models are slower and use more memory, and small
models can make mistakes. For the best answers, load a larger model via "Add a model from file".

Powered by Overfit (open source): https://github.com/DevOnBike/Overfit
```

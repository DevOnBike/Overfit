# Acme Software — Customer Policy Handbook

## Subscription plans

Acme Software is sold on three subscription tiers, billed monthly or annually:

- **Starter** — single user, community support, 5 GB storage. Annual billing saves 15%.
- **Business** — up to 25 users, next-business-day support, 200 GB storage, audit log export.
- **Enterprise** — unlimited users, 4-hour support SLA, dedicated success manager, on-prem deployment option, single sign-on (SSO).

All plans include a 14-day free trial with no credit card required.

## Refunds and cancellation

Customers may cancel at any time from the account dashboard. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period; we do not pro-rate partial months.

A full refund is available within **14 days** of the initial purchase for annual plans. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable but remain active until the end of the paid term. Monthly plans are not refundable, but cancelling stops the next charge.

If a customer was charged in error (duplicate charge, billing-system fault), we issue a full refund within 5 business days regardless of the 14-day window.

## Right of withdrawal (EU customers)

For customers in the European Union, the statutory 14-day right of withdrawal applies from the date of purchase. To exercise it, the customer contacts support in writing; the refund is processed to the original payment method within 14 days of us receiving the request.

## Data ownership and privacy

Customer data belongs to the customer. Acme does not sell customer data and does not use customer content to train shared models. On account deletion, all customer content is permanently erased within 30 days. Customers on the Enterprise plan may request immediate erasure with written confirmation.

Data is hosted in the EU (Frankfurt) by default. US hosting is available on the Business and Enterprise plans on request.

## Service-level agreement (SLA)

The Enterprise plan carries a 99.9% monthly uptime guarantee. If uptime falls below the guarantee in a given month, the customer is eligible for service credits: 10% of the monthly fee for uptime between 99.0% and 99.9%, and 25% for uptime below 99.0%. Service credits must be requested within 30 days of the affected month.

## Support response times

- **Starter** — community forum only, no guaranteed response time.
- **Business** — email support, next business day (Mon–Fri, 09:00–17:00 CET).
- **Enterprise** — email and phone, 4-hour response during business hours, with an emergency escalation path for production-down incidents.
